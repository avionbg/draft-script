Provide feedback covering these areas:

1. **Story structure** — Does the chapter have a clear beginning, middle, and end? Is there a central question or tension driving it forward?

2. **Pacing** — Are there sections that drag or rush? Point to specific passages and explain why.

3. **Character** — Are the character voices distinct? Is any character's motivation unclear or inconsistent with prior chapters?

4. **Thread management** — Does the chapter create, close, reopen, or advance any thread? Are any active threads being neglected too long?

5. **Continuity** — Do any continuity items in the context show up in this chapter? Are they handled consistently?

6. **Scene-level clarity** — Are there scenes where the geography, sequence of events, or character positions are confusing?

7. **Strongest moment** — Identify the single strongest moment and explain what makes it work.

8. **Priority fix** — What is the one change that would most improve this chapter?

Be specific. Quote lines where relevant. This is professional, honest editorial feedback — do not soften it.
