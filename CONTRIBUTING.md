# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in Draft-Script.

By submitting a pull request, patch, code contribution, documentation change, or any other contribution to this repository, you agree that your contribution may be incorporated into the project and distributed under the project's current license.

Please ensure that:

* You have the right to contribute the submitted work.
* The contribution is your original work, or you have the right to submit it.
* The contribution does not knowingly include material that infringes the rights of others.

All contributions are reviewed before acceptance and may be modified, adapted, or rejected at the maintainers' discretion.
